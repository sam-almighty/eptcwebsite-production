Sunday School

Jesus said, “Let little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these.” Mathew 19 : 14


By scripture we understand the importance of Children and as a church it is our responsibility to nourish them with wisdom and knowledge of the bible. This prepares them to face the world boldly and overcome the temptation of evil. They are the pillars of the future Christian society and messengers of the word of God.

With this above as mission, East Parade Tamil Church (EPTC) conducts Sunday class every Sunday from 8:30 – 10:15 a.m. It starts with worship service and departs to the Parish Hall, within the Church premises, after the 1st part of the service. This followed by praise and worship, then closes with scripture lessons in classes which is based on age group. The medium of instruction is English and if necessary Tamil can be included. Children are in-separable from chocolate which is served for them.

Children are encouraged to play music, sing song and take part in various competition conducted at inter Church level and Diocese level. There is also opportunity for the Children to enhance their organising capacity in the ‘Explore Talent’ competition conducted for other Churches in Bangalore by EPTC. Children can also develop and demonstrate their talent and skill in the annual day program and Christmas celebration. Annual picnic arranged to make the Children motivated and socialise with other children.

To make the learning systematic and structured there is syllabus, text books and examination conducted. The teachers and volunteers are highly motivated, dedicated, honorary and pious to give the best to the children.

It is privilege and honour to have your children in our Sunday school which is by Grace of God blessed with 175 children as of now. Please take this as our personal invitation for your children to be part of EPTC Sunday school.

Yours in Christ.
Sunday School, EPTC.
